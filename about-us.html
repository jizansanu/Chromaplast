About Us.
Who are we
Chromaplast is your one-stop destination for moulding and labelling services in South India. With years of experience in the labelling industry and advanced moulding capabilities, we offer a wide range of services all in one place.  We specialize in moulding and labelling, ensuring that you have access to a comprehensive set of solutions under our roof. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering quality and precision in every project Whether you need precise moulding for product design or eye-catching labels for branding, Chromaplast is equipped to deliver your needs. We value attention to detail and work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.
Why Us
Mission
Our primary goal is to optimize cost-efficiency for our customers by seamlessly integrating moulding and printing services under one roof and to become a global leader in Injection Moulding and Blow Moulding by consistently delivering innovation and excellence in plastic moulding. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of traditional moulding techniques by introducing cutting-edge effects and graphic enhancements that set us apart from our competitors.
Vision
Our vision is to revolutionize the moulding industry by becoming synonymous with innovation, quality, and affordability. We aspire to be the preferred choice for businesses seeking IML and BML solutions that not only meet but exceed their expectations. As we expand our global footprint, our vision is to provide our exceptional products and services to customers in multiple locations, fostering long-lasting partnerships and driving sustainable growth in the plastic moulding sector.
