Some Frequently asked Questions
Here are some frequently asked queries to us which would help you understand our products better.
FAQs
Are the plastic containers on your website safe for food storage?
Yes, the plastic containers we offer are food-grade and free from harmful chemicals like BPA. You can trust them for safe food storage.
Are your plastic containers BPA-free?
Yes, all our plastic containers are guaranteed to be BPA-free, ensuring they meet the highest safety standards for food and beverage storage.
Do your plastic containers come with a warranty?
Yes, we offer warranties on select products. Please check the product description or reach out to our customer support for warranty details.
Can I use your plastic containers to store both hot and cold items?
Yes, many of our containers are suitable for storing both hot and cold items. Always follow product-specific guidelines to ensure safe usage.
