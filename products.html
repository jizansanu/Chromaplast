Advantages of In-Mould Labeling
Durability
IML labels are long-lasting and resist wear and tear.
Seamless Integration
Labels become part of the product, ensuring a clean, professional look.
High-Quality Graphics
IML allows for intricate, high-quality designs and branding.
Customisation
Versatile for various products, offering different label sizes and shapes.
Efficiency and Cost Savings
Automated and streamlined production, reducing labour and time.
Environmental Sustainability
IML is eco-friendly, as labels and containers are made from the same material.
Consistency
Ensures consistent label placement and adhesion.
Compliance
Can meet industry-specific requirements.
Reduced Inventory
Reduces the need for pre-printed labels and label inventory.
